Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) and next-day delivery options are also available. Shipping times may vary during holidays or promotional periods.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition with tags attached. Some items like electronics have a 14-day return window. Final sale items are not eligible for return. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination but typically range from 7-14 business days. Additional customs fees or import taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and visiting the "Order History" section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ShopVerse gift cards. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.
